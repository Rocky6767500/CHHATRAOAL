About the Artist

Welcome to my artistic journey. I am CHHATRAOAL, a contemporary artist passionate about creating visual narratives that bridge the gap between traditional techniques and modern expression.

My work explores themes of identity, culture, and human connection through various mediums including painting, digital art, and mixed media installations.

With over a decade of experience in the art world, I have exhibited in galleries across the country and continue to push the boundaries of creative expression.

Specializations

  • Oil & Acrylic Painting
  • Digital Art & Illustration
  • Mixed Media Installations
  • Portrait & Landscape Art
